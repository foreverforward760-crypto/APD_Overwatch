The Sentinel System — Catching What Auditors Miss

Three layers of fraud and falsification detection built into normal workflow
💡 Key insight: the most dangerous fraud in group homes is not billing fraud — it is documentation fraud that conceals abuse and neglect.
🧠
AI Sentinel — Incident Language Analysis
Every incident report draft is scanned for "incongruent sentiment" — language that minimizes severity. "Minor touch" for a battery. "Small incident" for a documented injury. Passive voice that removes agency. The Sentinel scores the draft, names the specific violations, and blocks submission on a Flag verdict until the staff member revises or consciously acknowledges. The flag is logged either way.
Active
⏱
Time-on-Task Fidelity Tracker
Staff claim a 20-minute range-of-motion exercise. The task timer ran 4 minutes. The discrepancy is flagged, logged, and surfaced on the manager dashboard as a Fidelity Score event. Consistent flags trigger automatic BA review notification. This is the pencil-whipping detection system.
Active
📊
BA Effectiveness Index (BEI) — Fraud Detection
Every Behavior Analyst is scored on four dimensions: client progress, plan revision rate, plan accessibility, and overall BEI. BEI below 50 combined with stagnation flags triggers automatic APD notification review. "Uniform data entries" across plans is a Sentinel indicator. The radar chart makes the multi-BA comparison instant and visual.
Active
📍
EVV GPS Verification — Ghost Billing Detection
Every clock-in is GPS-anchored to the licensed facility address. Billed hours vs. GPS-verified hours are compared daily. Any discrepancy is flagged for CFO audit before the claim is submitted to APD. Shift overlap prevention blocks two staff from billing the same 1-on-1 service simultaneously.
Active
🛡
Staff Integrity Shield
Every incident report is immutably timestamped on submission. No administrator or operator can alter or delete it. Staff identity is protected in the audit trail — not visible to home operators. An anonymous APD bypass button allows direct reporting to the state, circumventing any operator pressure to falsify data.
